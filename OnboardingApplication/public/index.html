About Just Eat

Just Eat is a leading online food delivery marketplace, connecting consumers and restaurants through its platform in 24 countries.

Founded in Denmark in 2001, Just Eat has grown to become a global leader in online food delivery, offering a convenient and reliable service to millions of customers worldwide.

Our mission is to empower customers to choose from a wide variety of restaurants, providing them with an easy and fast food delivery experience.
